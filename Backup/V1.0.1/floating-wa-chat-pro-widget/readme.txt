== Description ==

<strong>The Floating WA Chat Pro Widget plugin allows you to easily add a WA chat button to your website. This provides a direct line of communication with your visitors, allowing you to offer support, answer questions, and engage with customers in real-time.</strong>

With a user-friendly interface, you can customize the widget to match your brand's look and feel. Add and manage multiple support agents, each with their own name, title, and pre-filled message.

**Key Features:**

* **Floating Chat Bubble:** A clean, non-intrusive chat button that sits on your page.
* **Multiple Agents:** Add multiple support agents and let visitors choose who to talk to.
* **Customization:** Change colors, button position (left/right), and button style (icon or icon with text).
* **Agent Details:** Assign names, titles, and pre-filled messages for each agent.
* **Pro Features:** Unlock the ability to add unlimited agents, assign departments, and upload custom agent avatars with a Pro license.
* **Easy to Use:** A simple and intuitive settings panel makes configuration a breeze.

Upgrade your customer communication and provide instant support with Floating WA Chat Pro Widget.

== Installation ==

1.  Upload the `Floating WA Chat Pro Widget` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2.  Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3.  Navigate to the 'WhatsApp Chat' menu in your WordPress dashboard to configure the settings.
4.  Add at least one agent with a valid WhatsApp number.
5.  Save your settings, and the chat bubble will appear on your site.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I add a new agent? =

Go to **WhatsApp Chat > Settings > Agents**. Click the "Add New Agent" button. You must provide at least a name and a valid WhatsApp number (including the country code).

= Can I change the colors of the chat widget? =

Yes. Go to **WhatsApp Chat > Settings > Display & Styling**. You can change the "Bubble Background Color" and the "Icon & Text Color" to match your website's branding.

= How do I get a Pro license? =

You can get a Pro license or request a 7-day trial key from the **WhatsApp Chat > Activate Pro** page in your dashboard. A Pro license unlocks features like multiple agents and custom avatars.

= Does this work on mobile? =

Yes, the chat widget is fully responsive and works great on all devices.
